The Three Badasses of The Expanse

I misunderstood the assignment entirely. I really thought I was supposed to just write 3 things about...anything. I was wrong.
Anyway, here are three dope characters from a sci-fi show I like.

from Earth
Chrisjen Avasarala
This cunning, sartorially blessed queen will leave you clutching for your pearls (and your self-worth) with three expertly crafted sentences and little else.
from Mars
Roberta "Bobbie" Draper
Trained by the Martian Marine Corps for just about any imaginable conflict, Bobbie leaves would-be assailants packing their bags. If they still have arms, anyway.
from The Belt
Naomi Nagata
The unflappable engineer of the Rocinante has a tool for every broken drive and a defibrillator for any spacecraft on the brink of death.
